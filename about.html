"Who are you?"

-That one lady at the end of Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker

My name is Alex Larson. Currently, I am finishing up the accelerated master’s program for GIS/Cart at UW-Madison (On Wisconsin!). I am originally from Steven’s Point, WI where I played football, wrestling, and track and field. From there, I went to Carroll University in Waukesha, WI (Roll Pios!) where I studied Environmental Science, and graduated in Spring of 2022. Outside of school, my hobbies include photography, finding new music, and enjoying time with friends and family. I’m always down for a good travel plan, just as long as my camera can come along!

Since my junior year of undergraduate studies, cartography has interested me, and through the program at UW-Madison I have really gotten to expand on my cartographic skills. To me, cartography is a perfect blend of science and graphic design, which makes it not only extremely powerful, but important as well! Feel free to explore my previous works and thank you for taking the time to get to know me!
